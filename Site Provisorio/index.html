Communicate through everything!

Tinkler is a new way for you to let people reach you, without having to share your personal information with the world.

Tinklers are objects tagged with our QR code allowing people to connect with you, easily, and with context:

pets - "I've found your dog"
bags or suitcases - "I've got your suitcase"
vehicles - "Your lights are on"
mailboxes - "You've got mail!"

and just about anything else you can think of!
Feel free to reach us for new thoughts and ideas through admin@tinkler.it
